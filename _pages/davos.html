Bright X Creative Media at Davos / .2020

Ashbright, the strategic communications experts, brings the Bright X Creative Newsroom to Davos, in association with Stromback, the #1 Davos networker. Bright X Creative Newsroom is the best way to maximise the return on your investment in Davos.

The newsroom – an integrated hub comprising live streaming video, social media management, high-end video production, journalism and photography – ensures that your success at Davos reaches hundreds of thousands of stakeholders worldwide, rather than just those lucky enough to be invited to your event.

At Davos 2020, Bright X Creative Newsroom will be available to a limited number of clients from our location on Davos Promenade.

Case study

Davos / .2019

At Davos in January 2019, we delivered a creative newsroom for Ukraine House Davos, showcasing the best of Ukraine across four days of discussion panels, celebrity interviews, cultural events and parties. The house itself saw more than 7,000 people visit over five days, but these numbers were significantly amplified through our work with live streaming video, original video production, live tweets and social media outreach. In those five days alone, we achieved 56,700 live panel viewers on Facebook, 83,800 fans across Facebook and Instagram and 110,600 page engagements. Numbers that continue to grow after the event.
